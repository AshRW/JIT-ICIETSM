About JIT

Jhulelal Institute of Technology (JIT) is governed by Samridhi Sarwajanik Charitable Trust (SSCT) with a noble cause of providing Quality Technical Education to the students in Central India. JIT is accredited by NAAC, approved by AICTE, recognized by DTE and affiliated to RTM Nagpur University. The society has eminent persons from the field of technology and education on its governing body and academic advisory council. JIT is situated in congenial nature setting 12 km away from the Zero Mile, off Koradi Road. The peaceful environment in the campus provides ideal atmosphere for academic pursuit, concentrated studies and research. The highly qualified teachers encourage students for conceptual learning and practical application of their knowledge. Students here are also groomed for life skills. Qualities like Problem solving & leadership are also looked after through activity based workshops and seminars.
We have also organized following International Conferences in past years
• International Conference on Industrial Automation and Computing (ICIAC-2014)
• International Conference on Science & Technology For Society (ICSTS - 2015)
• International Conference on Science & Technology for Sustainable Development (ICSTSD-2016)
• International Conference on Science and Engineering for Sustainable Development (ICSESD-2017-18
