About ICI ETSM 2019

International Conference on Innovations in Engineering, Technology, Science & Management – 2019 (ICI-ETSM-2019) will bring together experts from different streams of engineering, management and Science to discuss the contemporary issues, challenges, awareness, and innovations and their impact on Market. It will provide a forum for sharing insights, experiences and interactions on various aspects of evolving engineering technologies and patterns. The aim of the conference is bring together the Professors, Researchers, Technocrats and students in areas of Computer Science, Information Technology, Electronics Engineering, Electrical Engineering, Mechanical Engineering, Science & Management, which will be held in JIT, Nagpur during 27th and 28th April, 2019. The conference will provide an international forum for dissemination of original research, new innovations and ideas. All the selected and registered papers will be published in the International Journal.
